Content Operations

How I think and work

Good copy scales when it's built on systems. I design the infrastructure that makes consistent, localized, and maintainable content possible across teams and products.

Audits and alignment

I run cross-project content audits and document findings in Confluence. The goal: identify inconsistencies across products and align them to a single standard. The error state pattern in the portfolio is a direct output of this process.

Pattern library

I build reusable content components that design and engineering can apply consistently. Current patterns include error states, table copy, form labels, and empty states. Each pattern is documented with usage rules and localization notes.

Style guide governance

I'm currently leading the effort to unify product voice and tone with marketing at Turno. Marketing has an established voice; the product does not yet. Bridging that gap is the next project on my roadmap.

L10N workflow with Phrase

Phrase is a translation management system (TMS) built for software products. It connects to codebases, manages string files, supports glossaries and translation memories, and keeps content in sync across updates. I use it to deliver localized strings directly to the development team, removing the back-and-forth between writers and developers, reducing the risk of untranslated strings shipping to production, and maintaining quality across every locale. At Turno, this workflow supports 9 languages.
