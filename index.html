A localization audit of Turno's main marketing page identified multiple terminology inconsistencies, mistranslations, and style guide violations. Each change was documented against the primary style guide rule it addressed.

Issue Before After
Gendered term for cleaning staff Limpiador/a Personal de limpieza (collective) / Persona de limpieza (individual)
Inconsistent product term Lista de control / Lista de comprobación / Checklist Lista de tareas (standardized per style guide)
Industry vocabulary Administrador de fincas Administrador de propiedades
Critical mistranslation Pagos más limpios ("cleaner payments" as an adjective) Pagos al personal de limpieza

Trust signal vocabulary Investigados Verificados (vetted)
